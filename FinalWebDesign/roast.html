Night Kitchen


Roasted Coffee

All of Night Kitchen coffees are custom selected and freshly roasted to the
peak of aroma and flavor. Coffees orders are custom roasted just for you.
We roast on the weekends and ship at the beginning of the week in order to ensure you get
the freshest possible product.
Although we always recommend buying whole bean,
we are happy to grind for you.

Blended

Night Kitchen blended coffees use the same quality of micro-lot or small farmed
coffees as we use for our Single Origin coffees. Each coffee, by itself is a
great coffee. The trick is to bring together a set of flavors that is better
than the sum of the parts. It is a chance to experiment with how coffee
flavors interplay. All of our blends are batch roasted together.

Single Origin

Single origin coffees are ones that can be traced back to the farm or farms of
origin. These are all small lot sized coffees, typically farms that produce
between 4,000 and 5,000 pounds a year. Single origin coffees allow you to get
the sense of terroir of a well produced farm product.

Decaf

Night Kitchen brings the same care and selection we use with our regular coffees.
While it is hard to find a good decaf, when you do it’s worth the time spent looking.
