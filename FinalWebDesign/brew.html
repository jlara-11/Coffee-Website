Night Kitchen


Brew Gear

Simple, thoughtful, easy to use. Coffee doesn’t need to be complicated to be
good. Night Kitchen offers a small selection of coffee brewing gear
to help you take good coffee home or wherever you travel.

There is no reason not to drink great coffee, simply use a great roasted
coffee, hot water, and simple equipment.

Clever Coffee Dripper

This is the truth in advertising device in the coffee world. A deceptively
simple pour over cone, with a pressure based seal at the bottom.
Put in a filter, the coffee of your choice, and hot water. Then allow to
steep for 4 minutes. When you place the cone on a mug, the coffee drains
through. A clever combination of the best features of pour over and
French press styles. And to top it off, easy to clean up.

Aeropress (With Tote)

Deceptively simple, the Aeropress makes a tremendous pressure extracted coffee.
Great for travel, at work, or home. Comes with a travel tote and filters.
