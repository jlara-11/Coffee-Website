Night Kitchen in Chicago


Our Shop

Typically we roast coffee on Friday and ship the same day by USPS Priority mail.
Our coffee is all roasted to order. As a result, any order received after noon on
Friday will likely not be able to be filled until the following week.
We occasionally are at the roaster during the week and will do our best
however we are committed to providing the freshest possible coffee
when it ships out.

For orders larger than 3 bags of any coffee, please contact us directly.
We have a very small roasting capacity and need to schedule roasting across
all of our customers. We are flexible and should be able to fulfill larger
orders (within reason) and are happy to work with you.

We have discontinued the 250 gram size sample bags. We aren't happy with the
quality of coffee bags in this size that we've been able to get recently and
until we can clean up that issue, we will not routinely offer the smaller
size. If you are looking for a gift size, please contact us and we may be
able to help out in limited quantities.

Night Kitchen is interested in supporting your restaurant or business and has
very reasonable wholesale rates and minimum orders available. Please contact
us at orders@nightkitchencoffee.com or through the contact for on the
website to ask about our wholesale program. Night Kitchen coffees and spices
will give you a chance to offer your customers a unique small batch product
and further your unique presentation.
