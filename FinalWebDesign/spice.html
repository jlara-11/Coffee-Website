Night Kitchen


Our Spices

Night Kitchen is proud to offer a selection of custom and hand blends spice
mixes that are inspired by regional and world flavors. We have a mix of
familiar flavors with some unique flavor combinations that represent just
how global cuisine can be. We only use the highest quality ingredients,
sourced from the finest spice purveyors. We then hand blend in small
quantities. The process takes a lot of time but the results showcase our
ideas of the history of flavor.
