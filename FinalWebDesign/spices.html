Azteca Spice Blend

A mix of the traditional chili’s and spices found in a Mole Poblano, this blend
uses house roasted cacao nibs in place of the more traditional bitter
chocolate. A rich and fragrant blend, this is perfect addition to barbecued
chicken or ribs. Also delicious when used in a brine for pork chops. Let
yourself be creative.

5 Spice Powder

A staple in Cantonese cooking, Five Spice powder has spread throughout the world
with a unique twist at each stop. Our 5 Spice, ground fresh features some of
the original flavors but with a trip to Indonesia for a black pepper substitute.
This is a great spice to use in braising or cooking fatty cuts of meat
(duck, pulled pork, and brisket to name a few) but also works in desserts,
quick breads, and pickles. It has become one of our go to spices for many uses.
